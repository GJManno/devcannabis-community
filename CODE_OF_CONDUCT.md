# Code of Conduct

This is the public community space for [DevCannabis](https://devcannabis.com) — a project making
public cannabis regulatory data transparent and comparable. We want it constructive, respectful, and
genuinely welcoming to people of every background and experience level, in an industry that has too
often been stigmatized.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Help newcomers get oriented — everyone starts somewhere.
- Criticize ideas and implementation, not people.
- Keep it on topic: cannabis regulatory data, the project, and how to contribute.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks.
- Doxxing or exposing private information.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption or spam.
- Using this space to buy, sell, or solicit cannabis — this is a data-and-tooling community, not a marketplace.

## Enforcement
Maintainers and community moderators may edit, move, lock, or remove discussions and comments that
violate this code, and may remove repeat offenders.

## Reporting
For anything serious, contact a maintainer privately rather than escalating in public threads.
